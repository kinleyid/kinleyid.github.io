Plant game

In this game, you are a plant trying to grow tall and flower. This means growing new nodes. Nodes cost energy, and you need leaves to photosynthesize and get energy. If the plant gets too leggy, it might bend and snap. To avoid this, you can strengthen its lower nodes. There are also bugs that will eat your leaves. To avoid this, you can deploy your chemical defenses to wherever the bugs are eating.

Game mode
[V] drag view
[N] new branch
[L] new leaf
[D] defence
[S] strengthen
[F] flower
[R] remove node
Energy:
